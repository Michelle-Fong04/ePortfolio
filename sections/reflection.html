Personal Reflection

Technical and Personal Growth +
Technically, this course improved my understanding of data structures and algorithms, as well as how they are applied to problem solving. Through hands-on exercises and projects, I learned how to properly organize data, choose appropriate algorithms, and think more logically while solving problems. Personally, the training improved my communication, teamwork, and time management skills while also enhancing my confidence in taking on challenging jobs.
Challenges and How I Overcame Them +
During the group project, one of the most difficult tasks was coordinating ideas and responsibilities among teammates. Everyone had various perspectives and working styles, which occasionally resulted in misunderstandings or delays. To address this, we spoke more openly, specified our roles, and worked together to solve difficulties. This experience taught me the value of collaboration, adaptability, and effective communication.
Learning Through Interaction +
Interacting with my friends during class discussions, Padlet exercises and group projects substantially improved my learning experience. Listening to different problem-solving approaches helped me better understanding in data structures and algorithms.
My Contributions and Learning Growth +
By actively participating in conversations and group projects, I improved my grasp of the course topic. Explaining algorithm or data structure concepts to my classmates reinforced what I had studied, while receiving criticism from others encouraged me to enhance my thinking. My contributions helped both my personal learning and my peers' understanding.
Learning Journey in Class +
During class, I listened to lecturer and discussed with my classmates and participated in some games that were organized by the lecturer. The interactive learning environment made complicated ideas more understandable. Learning with my classmates increased my motivation and made difficult subjects more manageable.
Learning Experience with Lecturer +
Dr. Fajar performed a vital role in helping me comprehend data structures and algorithms. Clear explanations and helpful animation video made complex topics easier to understand. The encouraging approach enabled me to ask questions and actively participate in class, which enhanced my learning experience.
Future Benefits of Course +
The abilities and experiences I obtained from this course will help me in future courses and in my job. Many technical occupations require logical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a comprehension of data structures and algorithms. This course has taught me how to approach challenges more systematically and work more effectively in team-based initiatives.
Midterm Reflection +
In this session, I learned about basic data structures and why they are crucial in programming. I visualized data flow within arrays by doing operations such as rotating, moving, swapping, and so on. I learned about functions and how they help organize programs. When encountering difficulties, I discussed with friends or used online resources to improve problem-solving abilities.
Conclusion +
This semester, I discovered Data Structures and Algorithms to be both challenging and meaningful. Initially, I found the subject difficult, especially arrays, stacks, queues, linked lists, and trees. I needed time and practice to understand how they worked and how to use them in programming. I discovered how crucial it is to make programs more organized and efficient. The classes let me focus on understanding the topics rather than memorizing code. Making errors is a normal part of the learning process and helps me grow. Overall, this course helped me enhance my problem-solving skills and confidence in programming.
